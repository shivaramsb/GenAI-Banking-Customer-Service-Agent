# SBI Debit Cardholder Agreement

## 1. Usage Limits
The daily transaction limits for your debit card depend on the variant:
- **Silver/Classic:** ATM Withdrawal ₹20,000 | Online/POS ₹50,000
- **Gold:** ATM Withdrawal ₹50,000 | Online/POS ₹2,00,000
- **Platinum:** ATM Withdrawal ₹1,00,000 | Online/POS ₹2,00,000

## 2. Lost Card Liability
- The cardholder must report loss of card immediately to 1800-11-2211.
- **Zero Liability:** The bank covers unauthorized transactions made *after* the report of loss.
- **Pre-report Liability:** The cardholder is liable for transactions made before reporting.

## 3. International Usage
- Cards marked "Domestic Use Only" cannot be used abroad.
- International transactions are subject to FEMA guidelines.
- A markup fee of 3.5% + GST applies to all foreign currency transactions (except specific premium cards).
