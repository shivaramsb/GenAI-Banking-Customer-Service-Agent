# SBI Scholar Loan Scheme - Product Brochure
**Empowering Future Leaders**

The SBI Scholar Loan scheme provides financial assistance to meritorious students securing admission in premier institutes in India.

## list of Eligible Institutes (List AA)
- **IITs:** All Campuses
- **IIMs:** Ahmedabad, Bangalore, Calcutta, Lucknow, Indore, Kozhikode
- **Others:** XLRI Jamshedpur, ISB Hyderabad/Mohali

## Loan Limits & Security
| Institute Category | Max Loan Amount | Tangible Collateral |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **List AA Institutes** | Up to ₹40 Lakhs | **NIL** |
| **List A Institutes** | Up to ₹20 Lakhs | **NIL** |
| **List B Institutes** | Up to ₹7.5 Lakhs | **NIL** |
| **Above Limits** | > ₹40 Lakhs | 100% Tangible Security Required |

## Other Benefits
- **Repayment Holiday:** Course period + 6 months.
- **Repayment Period:** Up to 15 years.
- **Co-borrower:** Parent/Guardian is mandatory as co-borrower, but no collateral needed for List AA.
