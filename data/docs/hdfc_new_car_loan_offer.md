# HDFC Bank "Monsoon Bonanza" - Car Loan Offer
**Valid Till:** September 30, 2024

## 1. Zero Foreclosure Charges
For all new car loans booked during the offer period, **Zero Foreclosure Charges** are applicable IF:
- The loan is closed after 24 months from the first EMI date.
- The closure is done using own funds (not balance transfer).

## 2. Special Interest Rates
- **Sedans/SUVs:** Flat 8.70% p.a.
- **Electric Vehicles:** Flat 8.55% p.a.

## 3. 100% Funding (On-Road)
- Available for specific models (Maruti, Hyundai, Tata) for customers with CIBIL score > 780.
- Includes cost of Registration + Insurance.

## 4. Processing Fee
- Flat ₹1,999 (plus taxes) for all login files > ₹10 Lakhs.
