# HDFC Infinia Metal Edition - The Ultimate Guide

Welcome to the exclusive world of **Infinia Metal**, HDFC Bank's most premium credit card offering.

## 1. Metal Card Privileges
- **Unlimited Lounge Access:** Not just for the primary cardholder, but also for **Add-on** cardholders.
- **ITC Hotel Benefits:** Complimentary "Club ITC Culinaire" membership, offering 25% savings on dining at ITC hotels.

## 2. Reward Rate (The 3.3% Advantage)
- You earn **5 Reward Points** for every ₹150 spent.
- 1 Reward Point = ₹1 (when redeemed for Flights/Hotels via SmartBuy).
- This translates to a base reward rate of **3.33%**.

## 3. Golf Games
- Unlimited complimentary golf games at leading courses in India and select courses worldwide.
- Mandatory 24-hour advance booking required.

## 4. Insurance Cover
- **Air Accident Cover:** ₹3 Crores.
- **Medical Emergency Coverage:** Up to ₹50 Lakhs while travelling abroad.
- **Credit Shield Cover:** Up to ₹9 Lakhs.
