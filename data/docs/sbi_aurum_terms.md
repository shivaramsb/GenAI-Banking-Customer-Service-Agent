# SBI Aurum Credit Card - Terms and Conditions
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024

## 1. Membership & Fees
The SBI Aurum Card is an invitation-only program.
- **Joining Fee:** ₹9,999 + GST.
- **Annual Fee:** ₹9,999 + GST (Waived on annual spends of ₹12 Lakhs).
- **Replacement Fee:** In case of loss of metal card, a replacement fee of ₹3,000 + GST is applicable.

## 2. Benefit Usage
### Lounge Access
- **Domestic:** Unlimited access to domestic lounges in India.
- **International:** Unlimited access via DreamFolks membership.
*Note: Guest access is chargeable at $27 per visit.*

### Golf Privileges
- 4 complimentary rounds of golf per quarter.
- 1 complimentary lesson per month.

## 3. Financial Charges
- **Interest Free Period:** Up to 50 days.
- **Finance Charges:** 3.0% per month (36% per annum) on revolving credit.
- **Foreign Currency Markup:** 1.99% + GST (Lowest in segment).

## 4. Reward Points
- 4 Reward Points per ₹100 spend.
- 40 Reward Points per ₹100 spend on Tata Cliq.
- Points are valid for 2 years from accrual.
