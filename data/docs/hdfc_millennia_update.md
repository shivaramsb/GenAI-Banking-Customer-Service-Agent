# Important Update on your HDFC Millennia Credit Card
**Effective Date:** 1st April 2024

Dear Cardholder,
Please note the following changes to the features of your HDFC Millennia Credit Card.

## 1. Cashback on Amazon/Flipkart
- **Old Rule:** 5% CashBack on Amazon/Flipkart.
- **New Rule:** 5% CashBack remains, but will now be capped at **₹1,000 per statement cycle** (combined with other 5% merchants like BookMyShow, Cult.fit, Swiggy).

## 2. Lounge Access
- Comprehensive access is changing to **Conditional Access**.
- You must spend **₹1,00,000** or more in a calendar quarter to unlock complimentary lounge access for the subsequent quarter.

## 3. Wallet Loading
- 1% CashBack on Wallet Loads (Paytm, etc.) has been **withdrawn**. No points/cashback will be accrued on wallet load transactions.
