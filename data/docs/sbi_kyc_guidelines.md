# SBI KYC Guidelines for Retail Customers
**Document References:** RB/KYC/2023-24

## 1. Overview
Know Your Customer (KYC) compliance is mandatory for all account holders.

## 2. Officially Valid Documents (OVD)
For Proof of Identity and Proof of Address, any one of the following is required:
1.  **Passport**
2.  **Driving License**
3.  **Voter's Identity Card**
4.  **Job Card issued by NREGA**
5.  **Letter by National Population Register**
6.  **Aadhaar Card** (Masked e-Aadhaar is accepted)

## 3. Video KYC (V-CIP)
Customers can open savings accounts instantly via Video KYC.
**Requirements:**
- Original PAN Card must be displayed.
- Customer must be physically present in India during the call.
- Good internet connectivity and lighting.

## 4. Periodic Updation
- **High Risk Customers:** Every 2 years.
- **Medium Risk Customers:** Every 8 years.
- **Low Risk Customers:** Every 10 years.
