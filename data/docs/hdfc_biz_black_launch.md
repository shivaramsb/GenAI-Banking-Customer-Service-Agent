# INTRODUCING: HDFC Biz Black Credit Card
**The Ultimate Business Power Tool**

HDFC Bank is proud to launch the **Biz Black** Metal Credit Card, designed exclusively for Business Owners, Proprietors, and Self-Employed Professionals.

## 1. Core Value Proposition
"Separate your Personal and Business expenses with a card that pays your Taxes."

## 2. Key Features
- **Tax Payments:** Earn 5X Reward Points on Income Tax & GST payments.
- **Interest Free Period:** Extended period of **55 days** (vs standard 45 days) to help business cash flow.
- **Business Tools:** Complimentary subscription to Tally Prime and Zoho Books for 1 year.

## 3. Eligibility
- **ITR requirement:** Minimum ITR of ₹21 Lakhs per annum.
- Must be a self-employed individual or proprietor.

## 4. Fee Structure
- **Annual Fee:** ₹10,000 + Taxes.
- **Fee Waiver:** Waived on annual spends of ₹5 Lakhs.
