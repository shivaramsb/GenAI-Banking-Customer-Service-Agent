# HDFC SmartBuy - 10X Reward Points Terms
**Platform:** SmartBuy Portal

## 1. What is the 10X Offer?
HDFC Bank Credit Cardholders can earn up to 10X Reward Points (or 5% Cashback) when shopping via the SmartBuy platform on partner merchants.

## 2. Partner Merchants
- **Flights/Hotels:** Cleartrip, Yatra, MakeMyTrip
- **Shopping:** Amazon, Flipkart, Myntra (via SmartBuy link)
- **Train:** IRCTC

## 3. Capping (Important)
There is a maximum cap on accelerated reward points per calendar month:
- **Infinia:** 15,000 Points
- **Diners Black:** 7,500 Points
- **Regalia/Millennia:** 2,000 Points/Cashback
*(Note: Regular points continue to accrue even after the 10X cap is reached).*

## 4. Exclusions
- EMI transactions are **not** eligible for 10X points.
- Gold/Jewelry purchases on Amazon/Flipkart via SmartBuy are excluded.
